# Level 1 — The First Step

You are a warrior standing at the entrance of a dungeon. The stairs to the
next floor are somewhere ahead. Walk forward to reach them.

## Available abilities

- `warrior.walk()` — move one step forward

## Tips

The game calls your `turn(warrior)` function once per turn. Whatever you do
in that function is what the warrior does that turn. You don't need a loop —
the game handles that for you.

## Goal

Reach the stairs.
